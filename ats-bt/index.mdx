## Overview

The Echo ATS-BT is a high-performance Bluetooth 5.4 audio and data module designed to seamlessly integrate with the Echo ATS (Acoustic Test System). Featuring the advanced IDC777-1 module from IOT747, the ATS-BT provides comprehensive Bluetooth functionality for testing and measurement applications, supporting both Classic and LE Audio protocols.

The ATS-BT is ideal for developers who want to quickly and cost-effectively integrate high-performance Audio and Data Bluetooth functionality into their testing workflows. It supports multiple device connections with multiple profiles, including the new LE Audio Unicast and Auracast (Broadcast) technologies. With ultra-low latency (20ms) for Unicast and the ability to transmit 4 independent Auracast audio streams, the ATS-BT delivers professional-grade Bluetooth testing capabilities.

The module integrates with the ATS system via SPDIF digital audio connection, providing seamless audio routing for comprehensive acoustic testing scenarios.

## Technical Specifications

### Form Factor & Connectivity

- **Size:** 11.8mm × 22.2mm × 3.2mm
- **Weight:** 1g
- **Interfaces:** UART, GPIO, USB, I2S, PCM, I2C, SPDIF
- **Antenna:** Integrated chip antenna

### Bluetooth Specifications

- **Bluetooth Standard:** Bluetooth 5.4
- **Frequency Band:** 2,402 MHz to 2,480 MHz
- **Modulation:** 8 DPSK, PI/4 DQPSK, GFSK
- **Maximum Data Rate:** 3Mbps (typical 1.6Mbps)
- **Operating Range:** 25m
- **RF Sensitivity:** 0.1% BER at -97dBm (Typical)
- **Transmit Power:** Max 9dBm

### Supported Profiles & Features

- **Classic Bluetooth Profiles:** HFP, HSP, AG-HFP, A2DP Sink, A2DP Source, AVRCP, SPP, HID
- **LE Audio:** Unicast and Auracast (Transmit and Receive)
- **Ultra Low Latency:** 20ms Unicast
- **Auracast Capability:** Transmit 4 independent audio streams
- **Concurrent:** BLE and Classic Bluetooth operation
- **Multiple Connections:** Receiver or Transmitter modes

### Audio Features

- **Digital Audio:** SPDIF interface
- **Classic Codecs:** Snapdragon Sound (aptX, aptX HD, aptX Lossless), AAC, SBC
- **LE Audio Codec:** LC3
- **Speech:** Ultra and Super Wide Band Speech
- **Echo Cancellation:** Bluetooth, Wired, and USB
- **DAC Resolution:** 16 bits
- **DAC Sample Rate:** 8 KHz to 90 KHz
- **DAC SNR:** Class D typ: 105.1dB, Class A-B typ: 103.9 dB
- **Stereo Separation:** Min: 80dB

### Power Requirements

- **Supply Voltage:** 3.3V to 4.7V DC (Supports Li-Ion battery voltage range)
- **Streaming Current:** 5mA (Music streaming)
- **Idle Current:** <1mA (Connectable)
- **Discoverable Current:** <1mA (Pairing Mode)

### Operating Conditions

- **Operating Temperature:** -40°C to 85°C
- **Storage Temperature:** -40°C to 105°C

### Certifications

- Bluetooth SIG Certified
- FCC (United States)
- RED (Europe)
- MIC (Japan)
- KCC (South Korea)
- SRRC (China)

<Note>
**Audio Configuration:** The ATS-BT integrates with the Echo ATS system via SPDIF digital audio interface, providing high-quality digital audio connectivity for professional acoustic testing applications.
</Note>

The ATS-BT utilizes the IDC777-1 Bluetooth module from [IOT747](https://www.iot747.com/), providing advanced Bluetooth 5.4 capabilities including LE Audio (LC3 codec with Auracast and Unicast) and Classic Bluetooth with Snapdragon Sound (aptX, aptX HD, aptX Lossless) support.
