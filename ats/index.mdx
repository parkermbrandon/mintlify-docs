## Overview

The Echo ATS (Acoustic Test System) is Echo's next generation of test equipment for acoustic test and measurement applications. It represents the evolution of the modular Echo AIO product line into a single compact interface with improved quality and enhanced features. The Echo ATS interface, and its companion AIO-BT Bluetooth LE module, provide the capabilities most needed for production and QA/QC testing of consumer audio devices and their components. These include phones, tablets, speakers, micro-speakers, earbuds, headsets, amplifiers, and other acoustic / electro-acoustic applications.

## Technical Specifications

### Form Factor & Power
<Card>
- **Compact Design:** 11" W × 2" H × 8.5" D
- **Mounting:** Bottom mounting holes for stability
- **Power Supply:** Internal 40W AC/DC power supply
</Card>
### 8 MIC/LINE Inputs
<Card>
- **Dynamic Range:** 115dB  **THD+N:** -110 dB
- **TEDS Reading:** Full support for TEDS-enabled microphones
- **IEPE Support:** 4mA constant current source from 24V
- **Input Sources:**
  - BNC connector
  - On-board calibration signal (1kHz 1.0Vrms 0.1%)
- **Switchable DC Coupling**
- **Gain Settings:** Five input gain settings from +0dB to +40dB in 10dB steps
</Card>
### 4 Class-D Amplifier Outputs
<Card>
- **Power:** 10W peak, 5W continuous per channel
- **Dynamic Range:** 108dB (A-weighted)
- **THD+N:** 0.02% @ 5W, 1kHz
- **Output Noise:** 40 μVrms (A-weighted)
- **Impedance Measurement:** 0.1 ohm current sensing on Output 1
</Card>
### 2 Line Level / Headphone Outputs
<Card>
- **Dynamic Range:** 120dB **THD+N:** -113 dB
- **Two balanced outputs:** switchable for Output 1 / Output 2
- **Headphone Drive:** Can drive headphones ≥16 ohms
- **Output Levels:**
  - **LINE Mode:** Full scale 8 Vrms (balanced)
  - **HP Mode:** Full scale 3 Vrms (balanced)
- **Impedance Measurement:** 0.1 ohm current sensing on LINE/HP 1
</Card>
### Digital I/O (SPDIF)
<Card>
- Input switchable between SPDIF or Word Clock for external synchronization support
</Card>
### GPIO
<Card>
- **Inputs:** 4 TTL level inputs
- **Outputs:** 4 open drain outputs capable of sinking 250mA
</Card>
<Note>
**Output Configuration:** Outputs 1-2 are individually switchable between Class-D, Line, or Headphone configuration. Outputs 3-4 are Class-D only.
</Note>
