## Key Features

<Columns cols={3}>
  <Card title="Multiple I/O Configurations" icon="sliders">
    Flexible output modes including Line, Headphone, and Amplifier with programmable gain control
  </Card>
  <Card title="TEDS Support" icon="microchip">
    Built-in support for Transducer Electronic Data Sheet (TEDS) enabled microphones
  </Card>
  <Card title="Impedance Measurement" icon="gauge-high">
    Real-time impedance measurement capabilities for speakers and headphones
  </Card>
</Columns>

## Technical Specifications

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="Analog I/O">
    - 4 configurable outputs (Line/HP/Amp)
    - 8 analog inputs with programmable gain
    - BNC connectors for professional connectivity
    - Constant current supply for microphones
  </Card>
  <Card title="Digital Features">
    - S/PDIF digital I/O on BNC connectors
    - Word clock synchronization
    - GPIO for external device control
    - ASIO and WASAPI driver support
  </Card>
</Columns>
