## Key Features

<Columns cols={3}>
  <Card title="Modular Design" icon="puzzle-piece">

  </Card>
  <Card title="Advanced Measurements" icon="chart-line">
    Real-time impedance measurement, TEDS support, and environmental monitoring capabilities
  </Card>
  <Card title="Comprehensive Control" icon="microchip">
    GPIO control, battery simulation, and integrated pressure/temperature/humidity sensors
  </Card>
</Columns>

## Module Types

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="Audio Modules">
    - **AIO-A:** Acoustic test module with 4 inputs/2 outputs
    - **AIO-H:** Headphone test with impedance measurement
    - **AIO-L:** Balanced line-level I/O
    - **AIO-S:** Speaker test with amplifier outputs
  </Card>
  <Card title="Control & Digital">
    - **AIO-C:** GPIO, power supplies, environmental sensors
    - **AIO-T:** TDM digital interface (up to 10 channels)
  </Card>
</Columns>

## Common Configurations

| Model | Description | Inputs | Outputs | Special Features |
|-------|-------------|---------|----------|------------------|
| **AIO-SA** | Speaker + Acoustic | 6 Mic/Line | 3 Amplifier | Impedance measurement |

| **AIO-H2** | Dual Headphone | 4 Mic/Line | 4 Headphone | Dual impedance |
| **AIO-AC** | Acoustic + Control | 4 Mic/Line | 2 Amplifier | GPIO + Battery sim |
