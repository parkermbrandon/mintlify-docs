## Overview

The Echo AIO™ test system is a modular audio test platform ideally suited for high-volume production line testing and QA/QC verification. The AIO combines the functionality of multiple standalone devices into a single, integrated unit, making test stations both more reliable and less expensive.

The AIO Test System features a modular design consisting of an AIO Chassis, an AIO Interface Module, and one or two test modules, providing flexible configuration options to meet diverse testing requirements.

## Features

- **High accuracy** - Professional-grade measurements for production environments
- **Cost effective** - Integrated design reduces equipment costs
- **Silent operation** - Fanless design for quiet test environments
- **Standard USB 2.0 audio class interface** - Universal compatibility
- **Wide test & measurement software compatibility** - Works with industry-standard applications
- **Cross-platform support** - Runs on Windows (10 or later) or macOS
- **Multiple audio protocols** - ASIO, WASAPI, & Core Audio support

## Applications

- Speakers & headphones
- Mobile devices

- And more...

## Test Modules

The AIO system supports various test modules for different applications:

### A - Acoustic Module

- Four mic/line inputs with CCP, TEDS
- Two 10 W class-D amplifier outputs 

Ideal for comprehensive acoustic testing applications.

### L - Line-level Module
- Four mic/line inputs with CCP, TEDS
- Two balanced line-level outputs 

Perfect for professional audio equipment testing.

### S - Speaker Module
- Two mic/line inputs with CCP, TEDS
- Two 10 W class-D amplifier outputs
- Built-in speaker impedance measurement

 Specialized for speaker testing and characterization.

### H - Headphone Module
Two mic/line inputs with CCP, TEDS; two headphone/earbud outputs with impedance measurement. Designed for headphone and earbud testing.

### T - TDM Module
Digital TDM interface supporting up to 10 channels with 24 or 32 bit samples. Enables digital audio interface testing.

## Technical Specifications

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Microphone / Line Inputs">
    - **Input impedance:** 1 MΩ
    - **Input coupling:** AC
    - **Input gain:** 1x, 10x, and 100x
    - **Voltage, full scale (1x gain):** 8.75 Vpk (+15.8 dBV)
    - **Frequency response:**
      - ±0.01 dB (10 Hz – 22 kHz) (48k SR)
      - ±0.01 dB (10 Hz – 44 kHz) (96k SR)
      - ±1 dB (10 Hz – 86 kHz) (192k SR)
    - **Input bandwidth (-3 dB @ 192k SR):** 94 kHz
    - **Dynamic range (20 kHz BW):** 112 dB
    - **THD+N (1x gain, 20 kHz BW):** &lt; -105 dB (20 Hz – 20 kHz)
    - **Constant current supply:** CCP/IEPE/ICP, 4 mA
    - **TEDS reader:** IEEE 1451.4 Class 1
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Line Outputs">
    - **Output coupling:** DC
    - **Voltage, maximum:**
      - 16 Vrms (+24 dBV) (balanced)
      - 8 Vrms (+18 dBV) (unbalanced)
    - **Frequency response:**
      - ±0.01 dB (10 Hz – 21 kHz) (48k SR)
      - ±0.01 dB (10 Hz – 43 kHz) (96k SR)
      - ±1 dB (10 Hz – 75 kHz) (192k SR)
    - **Output bandwidth (-3 dB @ 192k SR):** 90 kHz
    - **Dynamic range (20 kHz BW):** 120 dB
    - **THD+N (20 kHz BW):** &lt; -102 dB (20 Hz – 20 kHz)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Amplifier / Speaker Outputs">
    - **Output coupling:** AC
    - **Load, minimum:** 4 Ω
    - **Voltage, full scale:** 9.475 Vrms (8 Ω load)
    - **Power output (20 Hz – 20 kHz, all channels driven):**
      - 10 W @ &lt;0.2% THD+N (8 Ω load)
      - 6 W @ &lt;0.3% THD+N (4 Ω load)
    - **Frequency response:** ±0.2 dB (10 Hz – 20 kHz) (8 Ω load)
    - **Output bandwidth (-3 dB @ 192k SR):** 44 kHz
    - **Dynamic range (20 kHz BW):** 100 dB
    - **Impedance measurement accuracy:** ≤ 0.5% (20 Hz – 20 kHz)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Headphone Outputs">
    - **Output coupling:** DC
    - **Load, minimum:** 16 Ω
    - **Voltage, maximum:** 3 Vrms (+9.5 dBV) (≥ 32 Ω load)
    - **Current, maximum:** 125 mA
    - **Power output (20 Hz – 20 kHz, all channels driven):**
      - 281 mW @ &lt; 0.0016% THD+N (32 Ω)
      - 250 mW @ &lt; 0.0019% THD+N (16 Ω)
    - **Frequency response:**
      - ±0.01 dB (10 Hz – 21 kHz) (48k SR)
      - ±0.01 dB (10 Hz – 43 kHz) (96k SR)
      - ±1 dB (10 Hz – 75 kHz) (192k SR)
    - **Output bandwidth (-3 dB @ 192k SR):** 89 kHz
    - **Dynamic range (20 kHz BW):** 120 dB
    - **Impedance measurement accuracy:** ≤ 1% (20 Hz – 20 kHz)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="TDM Specifications">
    - **Channels:** 2, 4, 8, or 10
    - **Clock source:** Internal or external
    - **Bits per frame:** 64, 128, or 256
    - **Bits per sample:** 24 or 32
    - **Frame sync width:** 1 – 128 bits
    - **Frame sync position:** Bit 0 – 255
    - **Sample rate:** 44.1k, 48k
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="General Specifications">
    - **Power:** 90 – 264 VAC, 50/60 Hz, 60 W
    - **Dimensions:** 17.5" (44.4 cm) × 8.75" (22.2 cm) × 1.75" (4.4 cm)
    - **Weight:** 42.5 lbs (19.3 kg)
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Software

### Test & Measurement Software

The AIO system works with a wide variety of third-party test and measurement software, including:

- **APx500 Flex** - Audio Precision analysis software
- **ARTA** - Audio measurement and analysis
- **LabVIEW** - Custom test automation
- **MATLAB** - Advanced signal processing

The AIO system works just like a standard sound card for Windows, Mac, or Linux.

### Control Panel Software

The AIO Control Panel provides comprehensive level monitoring and control over hardware settings, including:

- Transducer power management
- TEDS data configuration
- Gain control
- TDM format settings
- Calibration
- Command-line and API access to settings
